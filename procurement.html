What We Offer

Beam Maritime acts as a protective agent to represent ship owners' or charterers' best interests during port calls. We provide independent oversight and real-time updates, ensuring transparency and preventing conflicts of interest.

Our protective services include monitoring port operations, supervising disbursement accounts, attending cargo operations, and liaising with local authorities.
